About StudyBuddy

StudyBuddy is a tool that you can use in order to meet up with other people in your class. This webapp will notify you if a person in your class who is looking to study is in the same building as you. It also helps with finding a tutor for the class.

Through StudyBuddy, you can also get detailed information on the tutor, such as how often they are in the building and which days they are in the building the most often, using our state of the art visualization tools.
